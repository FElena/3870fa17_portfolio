Seasonal calendar : Fall

Instructor: Steve Jones

1 / 7
About the project:

Goal: Create a four panel calendar, using any theme and objects that support the theme.

I chose sweets to be the theme for a calendar for the 2017 year. My goal was to make the calendar cheerful, pleasant for view and have fun by experimenting with Photoshop. I decided to make the different associations with sweets according to each season. I classified sweets in four categories depending on their color, shapes, and frequency of sweets' consumption during the year.

Self Portrait / Initials Personal Logo

Instructor: Steve Jones

5 / 7
About the project:

Goal: Using my three initials, I created my personal logo. This self-portrait represents my personality and determines who I am today.

The letter E is the first letter of my first name. I made it the biggest in the self-portrait. It is because I see myself as an independent individual. I chose Edwardian Script font to show my individuality. I am delicate, easygoing and unique. I cut the part of the letter because I like to be different from others. I like to look out the box. The letter A is the first letter of my middle name. The letter comes after my father. I used the upper case to underline my respect to him. I flipped over f, which represents my last name, to emphasize the support that gives me my family. I put f into intersection line of E to connect all tree letters. F connects to E and A inside E. All of them are connected, my personality is the result of mixture of my first, last and middle name.

A Booklet About Tibor Kalman

Instructor: Steve Jones

7 / 7
About the project:

Goal: Create a cover page for the brochure about Tibor Kalman showcasing the style of the subject.

My chose of style, colors and arrangement of contents were influenced by Kalman’s works. His perspective was communicated through bold graphic design, typography, and juxtaposition of photographs and doctored images. Creating a cover page I was influenced by Tibor’s work, Talking Heads album, that featured four digitally manipulated photographs of the group members and a title with upside-down letters. I repeated his style and modified his photo. I used a color picker to use the same colors (blue and red) as Tibor used by creating the Talking Heads album cover. The Controversial Possibilities of Graphics I chose to be my title. I wanted to acknowledge Kalman's contribution to the exploring of the possibilities of graphic design
